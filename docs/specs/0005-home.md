# 0005 - Home

**Status:** Approved

## Goal

The screen after login: the active beneficiary's identity at a glance, shortcuts to the
most frequent journeys, and operator communication (banners and notices) managed as
content — the daily entry point of the portal.

## Scope

- Beneficiary card (greeting, plan, card number, avatar).
- "Acesso Rápido" carousel with 9 fixed shortcuts.
- Banner carousel with validity window and auto-rotation.
- Expandable notices (accordion, one open at a time).
- Seed content (2 banners, 2 notices).

## Business Context

The reference product (operator app) opens on a home that mixes identity, quick actions
and operator messaging. Banners and notices are operator-managed content: the POC loads
them by migration (no CMS), but display rules must behave as if content were live.

## Business Rules

- **BR1** — The beneficiary card MUST reflect the **active beneficiary** (SPEC-0003):
  greeting "Olá, {FIRST_NAME}" in upper case, label "PLANO MÉDICO", plan name, card number
  with icon and avatar (profile photo or placeholder); it MUST update immediately when the
  active beneficiary changes. Clicking the avatar opens the Profile (SPEC-0006).
- **BR2** — The greeting MUST use only the first name of the active beneficiary.
- **BR3** — The quick-access carousel MUST show, in this exact order: Reconhecimento
  Facial (opens the "disponível no aplicativo móvel" notice dialog), Guias e Tokens, Rede
  Credenciada, Telemedicina, Agendamento, Carteirinha, Minha Saúde, Canais de Atendimento,
  Alteração Cadastral (opens SPEC-0006 registration editing).
- **BR4** — Shortcuts to modules not yet delivered MUST render disabled with the hint
  "em breve" (supports the phased roadmap); enabled shortcuts navigate to their modules.
- **BR5** — The carousel MUST be operable by drag/touch and by keyboard arrows.
- **BR6** — Banners displayed MUST be only those `active = true` AND within their validity
  window (start/end optional), in the content-defined order; rotation every **6 s**, paused
  on hover/focus, with drag navigation and position indicators. The banner button navigates
  to its internal destination.
- **BR7** — Notices displayed MUST be only the active ones, ordered by content-defined
  priority; severity `alerta` MUST be visually distinct from `informativo`; the accordion
  allows only one notice open at a time.
- **BR8** — Unavailability of content (banners/notices) MUST NOT break the Home: the
  affected section is hidden and the rest works.
- **BR9** — Seed content: banner "Alerta de golpe! A operadora não solicita dados ou
  pagamentos por WhatsApp" (action → SPEC-0014 antifraud anchor) and banner "Valide seu
  boleto" (action → SPEC-0013 validator); notices "Instabilidade momentânea da
  Telemedicina" (alerta) and "Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados Pessoais" (informativo).

## Phased delivery (owner decision, 2026-07-04)

In the current phase, shortcuts and banners whose destination module is not yet delivered render
**disabled with the "em breve" hint** (BR4, extended to banner buttons) instead of navigating to a
non-existent route. Consequently **AC2 (navigate to the Carteirinha)** and **AC6 (navigate to the
antifraud section)** are satisfied **progressively**, when their target modules land (Carteirinha —
Phase 2; Canais de Atendimento / antifraud — Phase 5); the Phase-1 verified behavior is the "em
breve" state. The avatar is a **placeholder** until the profile photo arrives with SPEC-0006
(Phase 2). This phased-delivery note is the owner-approved record (owner decision).

## Input/Output Examples

- `GET /api/content/home` → `200 {"banners":[{"title":"Alerta de golpe!","text":"…",
  "buttonLabel":"Saiba mais","destination":"/atendimento#antifraude","order":1}],
  "notices":[{"title":"Instabilidade momentânea da Telemedicina","severity":"ALERT",
  "body":"…","order":1}]}` — a banner out of validity is absent.
- Content endpoint failing → Home still renders card and shortcuts (sections hidden).

## API Contracts

| Method | Path | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| GET | `/api/content/home` | Active banners (validity-filtered) + active notices |

Beneficiary card data comes from SPEC-0001's `my-plan` / SPEC-0003 context endpoints.

## Events

Not applicable.

## Persistence Changes

Migration (number at implementation): `banner` (id, title ≤ 80, text ≤ 280, image,
button_label, internal_destination, display_order, active, valid_from nullable, valid_to
nullable); `notice` (id, title, body, severity `INFORMATIVE|ALERT`, display_order, active)
+ BR9 seed. Severity is a fixed technical classification (two rendering styles), kept as a
constrained column.

## Validation Rules

Content is operator-loaded (read-only in the portal); the API filters by `active` and
validity window — filtering MUST happen server-side.

## Error Behavior

| Failure | Code (i18n key) | HTTP |
|---|---|---|
| Content unavailable | *(none — section hidden, BR8)* | — |

## Observability Requirements

Log content-load failures (BR8 path) with correlation id. No metrics required for the POC
(banner click metrics are out of scope).

## Tests Required

- **Domain/unit:** validity-window filter (before/inside/after window, null bounds).
- **Integration (Testcontainers):** home content endpoint returns only active+valid items
  in order.
- **API contract:** content endpoint snapshot.
- **Frontend unit:** card reacts to context change; disabled "em breve" shortcuts; carousel
  keyboard operation; accordion single-open; section hiding on error.
- **E2E:** login → Home shows greeting, plan and card number; banner navigation works.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **AC1** (BR1, BR2) — Given MARIA logs in, then Home shows "Olá, MARIA", plan
  "ADESÃO PRATA RJ QP COPART TP" and card `001234567`.
- **AC2** (BR3, BR4) — Given the shortcut "Carteirinha" is enabled, when clicked, then I
  navigate to the active beneficiary's digital card.
- **AC3** (BR6) — Given a banner whose validity ended, then it does not appear (error case).
- **AC4** (BR7) — Given the LGPD notice closed and the Telemedicina notice open, when I
  open LGPD, then Telemedicina closes and LGPD's content shows.
- **AC5** (BR1) — Given I switch the active beneficiary to PEDRO, then the card shows
  PEDRO's data.
- **AC6** (BR9) — Given the fraud-alert banner, when I click its action, then I land on the
  antifraud section of Canais de Atendimento.
